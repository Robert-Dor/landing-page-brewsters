Whether you're in the mood for a five star Prime Rib and Seafood Dinner in one of the comfortable and secluded booths in our Dining Room, a fine Champagne Brunch or al fresco Lunch on our patio, or a relaxed round of Cocktails and Appetizers at the Bar, we welcome you to our locally owned restaurant located in the heart of Old Town Galt. When you select Brewsters for your next dining or special occasion, we promise to provide you with a memorable dining experience.

We are also extremely proud of our extensive wine collection that specializes in local wines. Our cellar can be viewed through the glass flooring near our main floor bar.
